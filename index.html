What is Hot Beans Web?

Hot Beans Web is a company seeking young and aspiring new web developers to join our company and to grow and learn with us. We are a motivated and inspired company looking to help potential new employees find their footing in the world and making the daunting task of finding a job as a web developer a little bit easier.

Junior Web Developer – Job Specifications & Qualifications

Responsibilities:

  • Build and maintain websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Collaborate with designers and senior developers
  • Test and debug across different browsers and devices
  • Participate in code reviews and development meetings
  • Learn new tools and frameworks on the job

Required Qualifications:

  • Basic understanding of HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Portfolio of work (coursework or personal projects)
  • Good communication and problem-solving skills

Desirable Skills:

  • Familiar with Git/GitHub
  • Experience with frameworks like React or Bootstrap
  • UI/UX knowledge
  • Understanding of accessibility and SEO
